Frequently asked questions

Is there any fee for candidates hired through Glider app?

Glider charges a placement fee that is 4% of the candidate’s first year salary for hired candidates who independently apply for your job from the Glider app.


What happens after the 60 day trial?

You could make a payment and stay on the Professional plan. If we do not get your payment, we will automatically switch you to the Standard plan.
